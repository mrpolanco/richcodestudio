Widgets for everyday attention

Add Scripture to your Home Screen or Lock Screen in the size that fits your day. The daily verse appears automatically, and tapping the widget opens the full passage in the app.

  • Small, medium, and large Home Screen widgets.
  • Lock Screen widgets for quick daily reminders.
  • Deterministic daily rotation so everyone sees the same verse each day.

Curated by spiritual theme

Instead of random verses, the app centers on passages chosen for real seasons of faith: anxiety, hope, gratitude, forgiveness, rest, surrender, and more.

  • Eight thematic collections with 58 selected passages.
  • Public-domain Bible translations: ASV in English and RV1909 in Spanish.
  • Gracewell links for deeper prayer, Scripture, and reflection.
