Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Verse of the Day · Bible Widget, developed by Rich Code Studio, handles information.

Plain-English summary: Verse of the Day is designed as a simple, local Bible widget app. It does not require an account, does not sell personal data, and does not use advertising trackers.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app may store preferences on your device, such as selected language, selected theme, widget settings, and app state needed to display the daily passage.

Accounts and Payments

Verse of the Day does not require an account and does not include in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads.

Gracewell Links

Some screens may link to Gracewell, another Rich Code Studio app, so you can go deeper with prayer, Scripture, and reflection. Opening another app or website may be governed by that app or site's own privacy practices.

Tracking and Advertising

Verse of the Day does not track you across apps or websites and does not use your data for advertising.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can help address the issue.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to support@richcodestudio.com.
