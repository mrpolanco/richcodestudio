Verse of the Day Support

Help for daily Bible widgets, language settings, and Gracewell links.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Verse of the Day require an account?

No. The app does not require an account, subscription, or in-app purchase.

How do I add the widget?

On your iPhone, touch and hold the Home Screen, tap the add button, search for Verse of the Day, then choose a widget size. For Lock Screen widgets, customize your Lock Screen and add Verse of the Day from the widget picker.

Why is the verse not changing?

iOS controls when widgets refresh. If a widget looks stale, open the app once, confirm your language/theme settings, and give iOS a moment to refresh the widget timeline.

Can I use Spanish?

Yes. The app supports English and Spanish. It can follow your device language automatically, or you can choose a language inside the app.

What is Gracewell?

Gracewell is a free spiritual formation app from Rich Code Studio. Verse detail screens may link to Gracewell for deeper prayer, Scripture, and reflection.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure you are using the latest version of iOS and the latest app version.
  • If widgets do not appear, open the app once after installing it.
  • If the wrong language appears, check the app language setting and your iOS language settings.
  • If Gracewell links do not open, install or update Gracewell from the App Store.

Contact

Email support@richcodestudio.com with your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.
