What it helps you do

Himnario Celebremos makes it easier to access lyrics in the moments that matter, whether you are preparing for worship, practicing with a team, or singing devotionally on your own.

  • Find lyrics quickly during services or rehearsals.
  • Reduce friction for Spanish-speaking worship teams.
  • Keep the focus on singing, not searching.

Who it is for

This app is built for churches and worshipers who need dependable, accessible Spanish lyric support in daily ministry use.

  • Worship teams and church musicians.
  • Congregations using Spanish hymn and worship lyrics.
  • Individuals who want lyrical access for personal devotion.
