Himnario Celebremos

A digital Christian hymnal for churches, worship teams, and families.

Worship readyHymn lyricsChurch friendly

Keep the hymns you sing close at hand.

Himnario Celebremos brings a worship-focused hymn library to iPhone, making it easier to find lyrics, revisit favorites, and keep a trusted collection of hymns available for church gatherings, family devotion, and personal worship.

A hymn library designed to feel simple, familiar, and ready when worship begins.

What it includes

  • Complete hymn access: browse a large collection of Christian hymns in one place.
  • Favorites and quick return: save the hymns you come back to most.
  • Search and browse: find hymns by title, lyrics, or number.
  • Worship-friendly reading: a simple interface built for quick access during real use.

Best for

  • Churches and congregations
  • Christian families
  • Worship leaders and musicians
  • Students and teachers who need hymn lyrics close by

Himnario Celebremos is built to support worship, not distract from it.
