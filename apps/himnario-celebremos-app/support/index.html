Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add hymns to my favorites?
Tap the heart icon next to any hymn title to add it to your favorites. You can access all your favorite hymns from the Favorites tab in the main menu.
Can I create custom playlists?
Yes! You can create custom playlists by tapping the "+" button in the Playlists section. Give your playlist a name and add hymns from the collection.
How do I search for specific hymns?
Use the search bar at the top of the app. You can search by hymn title, lyrics, or hymn number. The search works in real-time as you type.
Is the app available offline?
Yes! Once you download the app, all hymn lyrics are available offline. You don't need an internet connection to read hymns or use most features.
How do I adjust the text size?
Go to Settings in the app menu, then tap "Text Size" to adjust the font size to your preference. Changes apply immediately to all hymns.
Can I share hymns with others?
Yes! Tap the share button (square with arrow) on any hymn to share it via text message, email, or social media.

Troubleshooting

App won't open

Try these steps:

  • Restart your device
  • Check if you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Delete and reinstall the app

Search not working

If search isn't finding hymns:

  • Check your spelling
  • Try searching for just part of the title
  • Make sure you're using the correct language

Favorites not saving

If your favorites aren't being saved:

  • Check your device storage space
  • Make sure you're signed into iCloud (if using sync)
  • Try restarting the app
