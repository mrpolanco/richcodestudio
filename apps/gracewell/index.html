Prayer, Scripture, reflection, and gentle spiritual rhythms for daily formation.

Daily prayer rhythm Journal + Scripture Guide + Together
App Store listing available soon

A calmer way to return to God each day.

Gracewell helps people build a prayerful rhythm through Scripture, journaling, guided reflection, and curated practices that feel grounded instead of overwhelming.

Come back to prayer, Scripture, and reflection with a pace you can actually keep.

What Gracewell includes

  • Today rhythm: morning, midday, and evening practices with a gentle pace.
  • Scripture reading: read the Bible, save passages, and choose your preferred translation.
  • Journal: write reflections, prayers, and carried items for the day.
  • Guide: Scripture-rooted reflection and prayer support with clear boundaries.
  • Paths and prayer packs: guided journeys for seasons like anxiety, gratitude, waiting, and rest.
  • Together: optional shared prayer, sessions, and spiritual rhythms with one trusted person.

Built with care

  • No ads and no cross-app tracking.
  • Offline-friendly Scripture and editorial content.
  • Optional iCloud-based syncing for shared Together features.
  • Clear crisis boundaries for the Guide experience.
