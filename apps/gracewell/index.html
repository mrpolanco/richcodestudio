What Gracewell helps build

Gracewell gives users a more sustainable way to build habits of prayer, Scripture reading, and reflection in daily life.

  • A daily morning, midday, and evening rhythm.
  • Scripture, prayer, Guide, and journaling in one app.
  • Shared spiritual practices for two trusted people through Together.

What is included

The experience is designed to feel clear, grounded, and genuinely useful for people returning to God each day.

  • Bible reading and curated passages.
  • Journaling, prayers, and Carry This Today.
  • Shared sessions and paths for faith formation with another person.
