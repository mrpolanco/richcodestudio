Why Gracewell exists

Gracewell is designed for people who want to return to prayer, Scripture, and reflection with a pace they can actually keep. Instead of trying to be everything at once, it focuses on a few daily rhythms and gives them enough structure to feel grounded.

Today Rhythm

Morning, midday, and evening practices that guide prayer, Scripture, and reflection without overload.

Guide + Journal

Scripture-rooted prompts, clear next steps, and a journal flow that supports reflection rather than just storing notes.

Together

Optional shared prayer, sessions, and paths for two people who want to grow in faith with a trusted partner.

What Gracewell includes

  • Daily prayer and reflection: start, pause, or close the day with guided practices.
  • Bible and Scripture reading: read passages, continue where you left off, and choose a preferred translation.
  • Journal and Carry This Today: keep prayers, reflections, and verses close throughout the day.
  • Guide support: Scripture-rooted reflection and prayer help with clear safety boundaries.
  • Paths and prayer packs: guided journeys for seasons like anxiety, gratitude, rest, and waiting.
  • Together features: shared prayer, sessions, paths, and story milestones with one trusted person.

Built with care and restraint

Gracewell is designed to feel gentle, not manipulative. It avoids ads, avoids cross-app tracking, supports offline-friendly content, and keeps the Guide grounded with clear boundaries.

Trust signals
  • No third-party ads
  • No cross-app tracking
  • Optional iCloud syncing for Together
  • Clear privacy, terms, and support pages
