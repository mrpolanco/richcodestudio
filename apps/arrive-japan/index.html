Learn before you go

Arrive Japan builds a practical route through survival phrases and beginner Japanese, with daily review designed around your trip timeline.

  • Hiragana and katakana drills with spaced repetition.
  • Slow audio and pronunciation practice for common phrases.
  • N5 vocabulary, kanji, and grammar in travel context.

Use it in Japan

When you are already there, the app focuses on quick, practical help: what to say, what a sign means, and what to do next.

  • Quick Speak emergency and help phrases.
  • Airport, train, restaurant, hotel, and convenience store support.
  • Manual sign lookup and camera-based sign scanning.
