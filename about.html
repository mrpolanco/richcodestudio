About the studio

We care about making apps feel clear, calm, and trustworthy.

Rich Code Studio is an iOS product studio focused on thoughtful app design, launch readiness, and the kind of details that make a product feel finished. We like products with a strong point of view and a clean user experience.

What we do well

We work best on apps that need more than code. That usually means product framing, copy clarity, interface refinement, subscription trust, accessibility, App Store readiness, and the many small decisions that shape whether an app feels coherent.

  • iOS-first product design and SwiftUI implementation
  • App Store launch prep, support surfaces, and policy pages
  • Interface cleanup, hierarchy, and conversion-minded UX
  • Product editing: scope, wording, prioritization, and polish

Products should not feel like prototypes.

We care about the things users notice immediately: the first screen, the first subscription decision, the first empty state, and the first time they try to come back tomorrow.

Good launches are structured, not rushed.

We treat support pages, policy links, accessibility, and App Store review risks as part of product quality, not as cleanup for later.

How we work

Clarity over clutter

We prefer focused features, stronger defaults, and fewer competing actions on screen.

Calm over gimmicks

Not every product needs noise, motion, or hype. A clear interface can be the differentiator.

Trust over friction

Subscription surfaces, settings, privacy language, and support links should feel honest and easy to understand.

Launch-ready by design

Accessibility, review readiness, and product copy are not extras. They are part of what makes a product ship well.
