What Rich Code Studio does

We help move ideas from concept to polished product, with a particular strength in iPhone experiences that need care, clarity, and strong execution.

Product Strategy

Positioning, scope, feature tradeoffs, launch sequencing, and the product decisions that make an app easier to understand and easier to market.

Native iOS Design

SwiftUI-first interfaces shaped around Apple patterns, accessibility, readable hierarchy, and polished interaction details.

Launch Readiness

App Store compliance, support and legal pages, purchase-surface trust signals, and the last-mile cleanup that helps a product ship well.

Studio philosophy Useful products earn trust faster than loud ones.

We favor focused feature sets, strong defaults, and interfaces that help people know what to do next. That usually leads to better retention, better reviews, and a cleaner launch story.

Good fit projects

Apps that need strong UX judgment, careful wording, App Store readiness, and thoughtful polish around subscription, trust, and daily use.
